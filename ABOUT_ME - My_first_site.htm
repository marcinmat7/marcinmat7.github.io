ABOUT ME


Hello! My name is Marcin and I come from Łódź (city in the center of Poland). Maths has been always my main interest. I graduated at Lodz University of Technology (2013-1018). I hold Master's degree of Mathematics. After gaining my experience in Commerzbank as being part of Credit Risk Validation team (2017-2021), I switched to Data Science path attending the postgraduate studies at Warsaw School of Economics (2020-2021). Currently I work in ING Tech PL validating non-regulatory models based on Artificial Intelligence and study Big Data at Warsaw University of Technology (2022-2023).


PERSONAL LIFE


I have a wonderful wife who makes me really enjoying the life. Besides, I love football (watching & playing) and table tennis. I am interested in savoir-vivre rules and love blueberry yoghurts.

SKILLS


My main area of expertise is mathematical aspects of Data Science. Statistical tests, convergence properties, bootstrapping methods - here I feel very confident. I also like experimenting with some models (see eg. Neural style transfer) and various cool DS projects. I worked with various programming languages (SAS, Python, VBA, SQL) but my beloved one is R. I also like teaching and sharing the knowledge with others :-)
